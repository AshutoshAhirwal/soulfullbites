FAQ

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within India only. We are working hard to bring SoulfullBites to a global audience.

Is your chocolate vegan?

Our "Dark & Bold" bar is 100% vegan. Our "Milk & Velvet" and "White & Rose" contain high-quality grass-fed dairy.

How should I store my bars?

We recommend storing them in a cool, dry place between 16°C and 20°C. Avoid refrigeration if possible, as it can dull the flavor profile.

Are your ingredients organic?

Yes, we use certified organic cacao, unrefined sugars, and natural flavorings. No additives or preservatives ever.
