Every bite is a question answered. Here are the most fundamental ones we've encountered.

The Foundation

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within India only. Each bar is packed with thermal protection to ensure it arrives with its soul intact, even in mountain heat.

Is your chocolate vegan?

Our "Dark & Bold" bar is 100% vegan. Our "Milk & Velvet" and "White & Rose" contain high-quality grass-fed dairy from local mountain farms.

"The best way to eat chocolate is with the eyes closed, and the mind open." — Anonymous Alchemist

Preparation & Care

How should I store my bars?

We recommend a cool, dry library. Between 16°C and 20°C is perfect. Avoid the fridge—extreme cold "shocks" the cocoa butter and mutes the story within.

Are your ingredients organic?

Absolutely. We use certified organic cacao from ethical estates, unrefined organic sugars, and botanicals we'd be proud to grow ourselves.
