The Mountain Codex — No. 02

Wild Lavender
& Mountain Ganache

A velvety infusion of 72% dark chocolate, wild-picked mountain lavender, and sea salt flakes.

Prep: 20m
Difficulty: Master
Yield: 24 Truffles

Ingredients

  • 72% Dark Chocolate 300g
  • Organic Heavy Cream 200ml
  • Dried Mountain Lavender 1 tbsp
  • Unsalted Grass-fed Butter 30g
  • Maldon Sea Salt Pinch
01

The Infusion

In a small saucepan, bring the heavy cream and dried lavender to a gentle simmer. Remove from heat and let sit for 10 minutes to allow the floral notes to bloom.

02

The Melt

Finely chop the dark chocolate and place in a glass bowl. Strain the warm lavender cream over the chocolate. Wait 2 minutes, then stir from the center outwards until glossy and smooth.

03

The Temper

Add the cold butter and a pinch of salt. Whisk gently until the ganache is cohesive and mirror-like. Set in the refrigerator for 2 hours until firm but pliable.
